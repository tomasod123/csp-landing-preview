03 · Why nothing has worked

You've been sold the same three lies by four different agencies.

The agency down the road. The agency your mate recommended. The agency that did a nice pitch deck. Different logos, same script. Here's what they all promised and why your revenue never actually compounded.

Lie 01

"You just need a bigger ad budget."

They doubled your monthly spend. Lead volume doubled. Your actual collected revenue moved maybe 8%. Half the new leads sat in a CRM for three days before a human touched them. Your cost per booked patient quietly went up, not down. You paid for more noise, not more cash.

Why it failed: the constraint was your pipeline, not your traffic. Pouring water into a leaking bucket produces a wetter floor.
Lie 02

"Here's your monthly reporting deck."

47 slides of cost per lead, click-through rate, reach, impressions, and a "paid social performance summary." A year in, you still can't answer two questions. Which campaign produced which dollar last month. What your patient lifetime value is by channel. Without those two numbers, you're not scaling a business. You're feeding an account manager.

Why it failed: vanity metrics let agencies look busy while your P&L goes nowhere.
Lie 03

"We've automated your whole practice."

They stitched five tools together with duct tape. Half of it broke after the second software update. The rest your front desk quietly stopped using because the automated messages made patients annoyed. You are still doing the follow-ups manually. The agency is still invoicing.

Why it failed: a pile of subscriptions is not an operating system.
