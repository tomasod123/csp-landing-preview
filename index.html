03 · Why nothing has worked

You've been sold the same three lies by four different agencies.

The agency down the road. The agency your mate recommended. The agency that did a nice pitch deck. Different logos, same script. Here's what they all promised and why your revenue never actually compounded.

Lie 01

"You just need more leads."

They doubled your ad spend. Your lead volume doubled. Your booked revenue stayed flat. Half the new leads never got called back. The other half got a text message three days late. You paid twice as much for the same number of patients through the door.

Why it failed: pouring leads into a broken pipeline makes the leak bigger, not the business.
Lie 02

"Look at these dashboards."

Every month the account manager joined a call to walk you through colourful charts. Cost per lead went down. Click-through rate went up. Your SEO rankings improved. Twelve months in, you still can't answer one question: which campaign actually paid for itself in cash collected?

Why it failed: clicks, impressions, and rankings are not a bank deposit.
Lie 03

"We've automated your whole practice."

They stitched five tools together with duct tape. Half of it broke after the second software update. The rest your front desk quietly stopped using because the automated messages made patients annoyed. You are still doing the follow-ups manually. The agency is still invoicing.

Why it failed: a pile of subscriptions is not an operating system.
